About this project


What we have created for you is for gamers of all ages and any background and for all walks of life. We have decided to create a platform for people who like to play Roblox. To get started select select your game of choice weather it be the most popular, most engaging, up and coming, top rated or top earning. After the selection, if user is unfamiliar with the game our web page will provide the user with several of the top videos from YouTube to give them a tutorial of how to play that game.Our site will be updated daily to make sure we bring the most up to date videos and information about the games to our users.


We have accomplished this by scraping information from the Roblox site for the most popular, most engaging, up and coming, top earning and top-rated games. We queried the YouTube API for the top 5 videos of the search for each game, and display each game with the top 5 tutorial videos for our service.
